Biography

I am a first year PhD student in Computer Science at University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign (UIUC). I am fortunate to be advised by Prof. Ravishankar K. Iyer in the DEPEND research group at the Coordinated Science Laboratory.

Before joining UIUC, I got my bachelor degree of Computer Science from The University of Hong Kong (HKU). I was advised by Dr. Heming Cui at HKU Systems Software Group. I also spent a semester as a visiting student at University of Wisconsin, Madison, where I worked in WiNGS Lab led by Prof. Suman Banerjee.
